Assessments

Video Interview

A web platform where job candidates record video interview responses after email-based authentication against Bullhorn. The system stores these videos, allows internal reviewers to evaluate them, and integrates with Bullhorn to retrieve questions and post review feedback.

Typing Assessment

Measures typing speed and accuracy through a 1-minute and a 5-minute test. Candidates have unlimited attempts to improve their scores, with highlights indicating errors and word progression. The goal is to achieve the highest possible words per minute (WPM) with accuracy. After completing both tests at least once, candidates can submit their best scores to Paramount.

Computer Assessment

Evaluates essential computer skills, including navigation of video meetings, web browsers, and basic system settings. Candidates answer 25 questions with up to 3 attempts per question. Includes a sample question to familiarize candidates with the assessment format.

Comprehension Assessment

Assesses understanding of key insurance concepts such as risk management, policy types, and policyholder responsibilities. Candidates receive a PDF study guide and must answer 30 questions based on the material. Each attempt offers a new set of questions, with up to three attempts allowed. Paramount will notify candidates if retakes are necessary.
